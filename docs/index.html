Features

Invisible to recording

Won't show up in screen recordings, Zoom, Meet, or any screen sharing tool.

Fully customizable

Adjust speed, text size, and font to match your reading pace.

Multiple screens

Works with multi-monitor setups. Place your prompter on any display.

Dark mode

One-time purchase

FAQ

Does it show up in screen recordings?
No. NotchPrompter is completely invisible to screen recordings, Zoom, Google Meet, and all other screen sharing tools.
What macOS version do I need?
macOS Sonoma (14) or newer. Works on both Apple Silicon and Intel Macs.
Does it work with external monitors?
Yes! Works with multi-monitor setups. Place the prompter on any display.
Do I need a MacBook with a notch?
No. It works on any Mac. The prompter appears at the top of your screen.
Can I place the prompter anywhere on screen?
Yes! You can position the prompter in the notch area, in any corner of the screen, or use the floating mode to drag it anywhere you like.
I found an incorrect translation

Can I get it for free?
Yes, NotchPrompter is 100% free and open-source. No trials, no subscriptions, no hidden fees.
How does voice-activated scrolling work?
It uses your microphone to detect speech. Text scrolls while you talk and pauses when you stop, matching your pace naturally.
